DHA Requirements
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Capable to ventilate Adults, and Pediatric (3 kg and above) patient population
* Ventilation modes: Control, Assist/control, BIPAP, BIPAP Assist or Bi Vent, NIV, SIMV, ASB, PPS, MMV, APRV, CPAP & apnea backup, close loop modes.
* Breath type: Volume & Pressure Control, pressure support, spontaneous
* Tube Resistance compensation
* Full alarm package
* Main stream eTCO2 monitoring
* Breath Rate: 0-80 breaths per Minute
* Tidal volume: 50 â€“ 2000 ml
* PEEP/CPAP: 0-40 cm H2O
* Short term built in battery back-up
* Built in blender
* Integrated nebulizer
* Adjustable oxygen concentration from 21 to 100% O2
* Monitoring of ventilator parameters and exhaled tidal and minute volumes, PIP, PEEP, MAP, Total Breath, rate and Real Time Airway Pressure, Loops, graphs etc.
* Weaning protocols options
* Suitable cart or trolley
